MALACCA CITY, MALACCA

City of histories and cultures

  • Visitors attracted with the architecture, foods and famous Jonker Street Night Market.

  • St. Paul’s church, A Famosa Fort, Stadthuys, the official residence of the Dutch governor are top attractions.

George Town, Penang

Top attraction in Malaysia

George Town is like an open museum with the British buildings, churches and Fort Cornwallis.
Famous for the foods like Nasi Kandar, Pasembor and Mee Sotong.

The Perhentian, Terengganu

One of the most beautiful places in Malaysia to visit, Located at the South China Sea off the coast of Terengganu
  • Coral-Surrounded island.
  • White sand and crystal clear shallow water lapping against the shore.
  • Perfect for scuba diving, snorkelling, canoeing.
  • Visitors should avoid the area during the Eastern Monsoon between March and October.

Pulau Tioman, Pahang

The most beautiful island in the year of 1970s, Located off the east coast of the South China Sea

Rich coral, untouched beach, interior filled with dense rainforest. Suggested activities are such as scuba diving, snorkelling, trekking in the jungle, visiting waterfall.
