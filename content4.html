Top Malaysia East cost zone signature dishes

NASI DAGANG

Terengganu, Malaysia

Terengganu commercial rice is usually made from a mixture of plain rice and glutinous rice and cooked(steam) with coconut milk. As for the side dishes, nasi dagang will be served with gravy cooked using special spices. The fish used are usually cod. In addition, cucumber pickles are also served. Nasi dagang on the day is usually a breakfast dish for the people of Terengganu and also a special dish for certain events. .

NASI KERABU

Kelantan, Malaysia

Nasi Kerabu is a Malay delicacy of Kelantan origin. A similar rice dish popular in southern Thailand, known as khao jam or khao yum, can be traced to Nasi kerabu. In the condiments, flavoring and color of the rice, Khao jam varies from nasi kerabu; blue rice is a Kelantanese choice.
A complete nasi kerabu is a complex dish involving various components: the rice, herb salad, a coconut and fish relish, a spicy sambal sauce or two and various condiments, each an elaborate recipe on their own if you were to make them from scratch: salted duck egg, keropok ikan (fish cracker), solok lada (stuffed green chilli) and fried fish or chicken.
